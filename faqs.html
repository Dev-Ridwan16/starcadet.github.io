Do you sell all products
YES! Starcadet sells more than 12 million products, not including books, media, wine and services. When starcadet Marketplace sellers are factored in, the total product count balloons to more than 353 million.
Can someone sell on starcadet
YES! You can use Starcadet to sell certain products and help improve brand awareness, as well as create an online store where you control the experience and the data, and save money on fees.
How can someone sell on starcadet
Creating an individual seller account on Starcadet costs you absolutely nothing. All you need is to give Starcadet a little information and you're good to go. List your product on Starcadet for free.
Do you ship all over the countries
YES! Shipping will be a little more and the wait time is usually around three weeks. Some countries charge customs fees, in which case the receiver will be responsible to cover that cost upon arrival. The best way to find out if we ship to your country is to add the items you want to the cart and fill in your shipping information.
